// Common Questions

Straight answers before you apply.

What exactly is a merchant cash advance?

A merchant cash advance gives you a lump sum of capital upfront in exchange for a fixed percentage of your future sales. Instead of a rigid monthly payment, repayment scales with your revenue — lighter during slow stretches, which suits seasonal and project-based work.

Will applying hurt my credit score?

No. Checking your funding offer uses a soft inquiry, which has no impact on your credit score. A hard inquiry only happens if you choose to move forward with funding.

How fast can I actually get funded?

Most applicants receive a decision the same business day. Once you accept an offer and complete signing, funds typically reach your account within 24 hours.

My credit isn't great. Can I still qualify?

Very likely, yes. We weigh the health of your business — revenue, bank activity, time in operation — far more heavily than a credit score. Past credit challenges rarely disqualify a business on their own.

What are the basic requirements?

Generally we look for at least 6 months in business, $15,000 or more in average monthly revenue, and an active business bank account.
