// Common Questions

Things businesses ask before applying.

What exactly is a merchant cash advance?

A merchant cash advance gives you a lump sum of capital upfront in exchange for a fixed percentage of your future sales. Instead of a fixed monthly payment, repayment automatically scales with your revenue — so payments are lighter during slow periods.

Will applying hurt my credit score?

No. Checking your funding offer with Stone Cold Loans uses a soft inquiry, which has no impact on your credit score. You'll only see a hard inquiry if you choose to move forward with funding.

How fast can I actually get funded?

Most applicants receive a decision the same business day. Once you accept an offer and complete signing, funds typically reach your account within 24 hours.

What are the basic requirements?

Generally we look for at least 6 months in business, $15,000 or more in average monthly revenue, and an active business bank account. Credit history is considered but is rarely a disqualifier on its own.

How is repayment handled?

Repayment is collected as a small, agreed-upon percentage of your daily or weekly sales, drawn automatically. Because it's tied to revenue, your payment naturally adjusts with your business activity.
